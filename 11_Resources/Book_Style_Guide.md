# Book Style Guide

## Book Title

Mastering Burp Suite

A Practical Hands-On Guide for Beginners

---

## Author

Henry Uwaezuoke

---

## Series

Henry Uwaezuoke Cybersecurity Series

---

## Motto

Learn. Practice. Secure.

---

## Writing Style

Write like a mentor sitting beside the reader.

Never assume prior knowledge.

Explain technical concepts using simple language before introducing advanced terminology.

Every chapter must be practical.

---

## Chapter Standard

Every chapter contains:

- Difficulty
- Estimated Time
- Skills Learned
- Learning Objectives
- Why This Chapter Matters
- Theory
- Hands-on Lab
- Screenshot Walkthrough
- From My Lab
- Common Beginner Mistakes
- Troubleshooting
- SOC Analyst Note
- Career Connection
- Security Tip
- Practical Reflection
- Review Questions
- Chapter Summary
- Henry's Insight
- What You've Accomplished
- Next Chapter Preview

---

## Figure Standard

Every screenshot must have:

Figure Number

Screenshot Filename

Explanation

## Screenshot Rule

Never insert a screenshot without explaining what the reader should notice.

## Author Rule

Never encourage unauthorized testing.
